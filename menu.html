Daniel’s was the first to introduce the Philippines’ first broadcast studio-café in 2016. The concept came about when veteran broadcaster Kuya Daniel Razon and his lovely and talented wife Arlene envisioned a coffee shop that fosters conversations beyond the confines of a table aside from serving creative coffee creations, delightful beverages, and sumptuous pastries
