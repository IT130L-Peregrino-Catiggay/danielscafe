About Us



Daniel’s was the first to introduce the Philippines’ first broadcast
studio-café in 2016. The concept came about when veteran
broadcaster Kuya Daniel Razon and his lovely and talented wife
Arlene envisioned a coffee shop that fosters conversations beyond
the confines of a table aside from serving creative coffee creations,
delightful beverages, and sumptuous pastries.


The coffee shop is home to the public affairs program Get It Straight
with Daniel Razon and Daniel's Presents, an interactive musical
experience for music artists and stand-up comic artists with open mic
sessions for the audience.
